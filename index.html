Pizza Hut is the world's largest pizza chain, founded in 1958 in Wichita, Kansas by brothers Dan and Frank Carney. It has over 16,000 locations worldwide and offers Italian-American cuisine like pizza and pasta. Pizza Hut has several restaurant formats including dine-in, delivery/carry-out, and hybrid locations. It also operates express locations and has introduced an upscale "Bistro" concept menu. Pizza Hut is now a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, one of the largest restaurant companies in the world
